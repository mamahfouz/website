About me
======
Member of an academic research group led by Prof. Manuela Veloso and Prof. Tucker Balch focusing on the applications of machine learning in finance. My research focuses on the use of reinforcement learning and agent-based modelling (ABM) for limit order book trading applications.

Research Interests
======
1. Optimal Trade Execution
1. Market Making
1. Agent-based modelling
